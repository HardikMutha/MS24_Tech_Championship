TECHNICAL CHAMPIONSHIP

Overview

Technical Championship is a prestigious award presented to the college that demonstrates overall excellence across all the competitions in MindSpark'24. The title is awarded based on the cumulative points system, with each competition contributing towards the final tally. Technical Championship is a Rolling Trophy awarded to the college based on the performance of their students in different competitions organized by MindSpark'24.

Point Allocation & TIE-BREAKS

If a team consists of participants from more than one college, each participating college will be awarded the full points earned by the team.

TIE-BREAKING CRITERIA

In the case of identical scores of two or more colleges at the conclusion of all the competitions, the following Tie-Breaker criteria shall be applied sequentially:

  1. Number of first Place Finishes

    The college with the greater number of 1st place finishes across all competitions shall be declared the winner.

  2. Number of second place finishes

    If the tie remains after applying the first criterion, the college with the greater number of 2nd place finishes across all competitions shall be declared the winner.

  3. Number of third place finishes

    If the tie persists after applying second criterion, the college with the greater number of 3rd place finishes across all competitions shall be declared the winner.

  4. In the event all three criteria have been applied and there is still a tie, MindSpark’24 team reserves the right to introduce a further criterion or award co-winners if required, at its own discretion.

Rules

  1. All participants must have a valid college ID and Registration receipt.
  2. Participation in the workshops or webinars will not be counted for participation points
  3. Each competition will have its own specific rules, but the overall scoring system will follow the guidelines provided above.
  4. No points will be awarded for any competition where a participant or team fails to participate due to late arrival or late submission and no appeals will be entertained regarding this matter. No exceptions will be made under any circumstances.
  5. Any misbehaviour or violation of code of conduct of MindSpark’24 will lead to disqualification from Technical Championship.
  6. All decisions made by the MindSpark’24 are final and binding. No appeals regarding the subjective judgment of performance will be entertained.
  7. If any participant or team is caught engaging in cheating, use of unauthorized devices or resources, tampering with other participants' work or receiving external help during the competition, it will lead to direct disqualification of the team/participant.
  8. The MindSpark’24 team reserves the right to change any or all the above rules as they deem fit
